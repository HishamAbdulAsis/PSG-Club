ACHIEVEMENTS

ACHIEVEMENTS

Ligue 1:
PSG has won the French top-flight league title a record 10 times, most recently in the 2020-21 season.

Coupe de France:
PSG has won the French Cup a record 14 times, most recently in the 2020-21 season.

Coupe de la Ligue:
PSG has won the French League Cup a record 9 times, most recently in the 2019-20 season.

UEFA Cup Winners' Cup:
PSG won the European Cup Winners' Cup in 1996 after beating Rapid Vienna 1-0 in the final.

UEFA Champions League:
PSG reached the final of the UEFA Champions League for the first time in their history in the 2019-20 season, where they were beaten 1-0 by Bayern Munich.

UEFA Europa League:
PSG reached the quarterfinals of the UEFA Europa League twice, in the 1996-97 and 2009-10 seasons.
