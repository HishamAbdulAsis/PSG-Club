ABOUT US

Paris Saint-Germain Football Club, commonly referred to as PSG, is a French professional football club based in the city of Paris. The club was founded in 1970, following the merger of Paris FC and Stade Saint-Germain. PSG has since grown into one of the most successful and popular football clubs in France, with a dedicated fan base both within the country and internationally.

Over the years, PSG has won numerous domestic and international titles, including 10 Ligue 1 championships, 14 Coupe de France titles, and 9 Coupe de la Ligue titles. In addition, the club has also achieved significant success in European competitions, including reaching the UEFA Champions League final in 2020.

One of the key factors in PSG's success has been its ability to attract top-level talent from around the world. The club has signed numerous world-class players over the years, including Neymar, Kylian Mbappe, and Lionel Messi, among others. These players, along with a talented and experienced coaching staff, have helped the club establish itself as a dominant force in French and European football.

Off the field, PSG is known for its dedication to promoting diversity and inclusion in all aspects of the club. The club has launched several initiatives aimed at promoting gender equality, combating racism, and supporting youth development programs. PSG has also established a strong partnership with the Qatar Foundation, which has helped the club grow both domestically and internationally.

Overall, PSG is a highly successful and respected football club that has earned a loyal following both within France and around the world. With a talented and experienced roster of players, along with a dedicated and passionate fan base, PSG is sure to remain a dominant force in the world of football for years to come.
